Instructions

On desktop, you can press the left and right arrow keys to move forward/backward through the photos. Moving the mouse over a live photo will play the short video content. Panoramas don't have a live video part. Some regular photos also don't have video, but most do.

Live photos will play the video automatically the first time; so that will tell you if there is a live video part.

On a cellphone, you can swipe left or right to advance through the photos. Pressing a photo will show the live video content.
